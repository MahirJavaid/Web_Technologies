Pak vs Eng T20 series

The T20 series between two world champions, Pakistan and England, is going to start soon. The first T20I between the two teams will be played on 22 May 2024, in Leeds.

UEFA Champions League Final

The UEFA Champions League Final 2024 is set to be played on 2 June 2024 in London. The two finalists were confirmed as Borussia Dortmund defeated Paris Saint German in semi final, and Real Madrid completed another dream comeback to beat Bayern Munich. Both will face of each other and it is guaranted to be a spectacle for the fans.

ICC T20 WC 2024

The ICC T20 World Cup 2024 is set to commence from 2 June 2024. The countdown for fans has already started. Warm up fixtures have also been announced. This will be the biggest T20 World Cup in history featuring 20 teams.

Real Madrid Crowned Laliga Champions

Following their victory against Cadiz and as a result of the match between Barcelona and Girona in which the latter defeated the last year champions, Real Madrid have won the Laliga title for record 36th time in the club's history, which is by far the most for any other Spanish club.

IPL 2024 Playoffs Confirmed

After all the results from last week, the teams are confirmed that will be contesting for the trophy this year. KKR, RR, and SRH were already through and after defating CSK in their last group game at home, RCB becomes the fourth team to qualify for playoffs this year. Note that the playoffs of IPL will commence from 21 May 2024.

Four Peat Champions: Manchester City

With a comprehensive 3-1 victory over West Ham, Manchester City were successfull in defending their Premier League title. Moreover, City became the first team to win the Premier League four times in a row as Arsenal fell short for second time in 2 years, only by a mere margin of 2 points.
